Project Description

Eureka is a team consisting of three individuals focused on developing a website related to mental health. DialogDiri is a website being developed by Eureka, and it is currently in the development stage. DialogDiri aims to be a place for individuals facing mental health issues, particularly for the younger generation. DialogDiri offers services such as mental health issue consultations through a chatbot and consultations with a psychologist. We also have features like mental health tests and a chatbot to assist you.
