Project Description

SellQueen is a team of three members focused on creating a web platform designed specifically for thrift enthusiasts. Our platform enables thrift lovers not only to purchase affordable items but also to sell their own belongings. We serve as the primary destination for those seeking budget-friendly and unique items and for those who wish to share their collections with the thrift community.
