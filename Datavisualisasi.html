Project Description

In the project of visualizing natural disaster data in West Java, we are a team consisting of two members. For this project, we utilized natural disaster data in West Java from 2020 to 2022 as our analytical material. We employed Tableau as our primary tool to create informative and effective visualizations. The objective of this project is to provide better insights into the patterns of natural disasters in the region, thereby aiding in improved mitigation and preparedness efforts for the future.
