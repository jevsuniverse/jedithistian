Project Description

The AR Binus Malang application is a project by three individuals aimed at maximizing the use of digital technology as a highly beneficial promotional tool. The development of the AR Binus Malang Building application aims to expand information about the BINUS Malang campus in the current digital era. From the formulation of this idea, the concept of creating Augmented Reality (AR) that displays information about BINUS Malang's departments, history, and 3D buildings was created.
